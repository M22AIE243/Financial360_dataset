The APD Quarterly Volume chart displays significant fluctuations with an overall increasing trend from Q1 2023 to Q4 2024. Volume initially declined in 2023, reaching a low in Q3, before experiencing a strong recovery by year-end.

This momentum carried into Q1 2024, where volume peaked at approximately 110 million units, the highest point observed. While volumes moderated somewhat in the latter half of 2024, they remained substantially above 2023 levels, indicating strong year-over-year growth for APD.
