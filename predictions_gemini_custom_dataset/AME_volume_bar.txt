This chart displays AME's quarterly volume from Q1 2023 through Q4 2024. Volume remained relatively stable throughout 2023, hovering between approximately 5.8 million and 6.3 million units per quarter.

However, a noticeable increase in volume began in Q2 2024, continuing to rise sharply. The company achieved its highest volume in Q3 2024, reaching close to 7.9 million units. This robust performance carried into Q4 2024, which also saw strong volume around 7.7 million units, significantly above 2023 levels.
