The EXPE stock price displayed a strong overall bullish trend from early 2023 to early 2025, more than doubling its value from approximately $90 to over $185. The period saw an initial upward movement followed by consolidation through much of 2023.

A significant rally occurred in late 2023, pushing the price to around $160 by early 2024, which was followed by a substantial correction into mid-2024. However, the stock initiated another robust and sustained rally from late 2024, reaching new multi-year highs above $180 by the beginning of 2025.
