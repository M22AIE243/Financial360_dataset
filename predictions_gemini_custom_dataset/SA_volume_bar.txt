The SA Quarterly Volume demonstrated a significant upward trend from Q1 2023 through Q4 2024. After an initial increase, volume saw a dip to approximately 17 million in Q3 2023, followed by a strong rebound to over 27 million by Q4 2023.

This positive momentum accelerated throughout 2024, with volumes consistently growing each quarter, reaching a peak of approximately 37 million in Q4 2024. Overall, the quarterly volume more than doubled from its lowest point in Q3 2023 to its highest point in Q4 2024, indicating robust expansion.
