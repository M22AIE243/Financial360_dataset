This chart depicts FMC's quarterly volume from Q1 2023 through Q4 2024. The company experienced robust volume growth throughout 2023, culminating in a peak of approximately 124 million in Q1 2024.

Following this high, volumes saw a significant decline in the latter half of 2024, dropping to roughly 81 million by Q4 2024. While the year-end 2024 volume is still higher than early 2023 levels, it represents a substantial decrease from the peak performance seen earlier in the year.
