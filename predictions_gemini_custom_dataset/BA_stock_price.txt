The BA stock price showed considerable volatility from early 2023 to early 2025. After fluctuating around $200-$220 for much of 2023, it surged to a peak of over $260 by early 2024.

This peak was followed by a sharp and sustained decline throughout 2024, dropping to a low near $140 by November. The stock then demonstrated a modest recovery, ending early 2025 at approximately $180, which is below its starting price in early 2023.
