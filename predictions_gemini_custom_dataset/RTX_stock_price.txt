The RTX stock price experienced significant volatility between early 2023 and early 2025. After initially fluctuating around $90-$95, the stock saw a sharp decline in Q3 2023, dropping to a low near $68.

A robust recovery and strong rally followed throughout 2024, propelling the stock past its previous highs to peak above $120. By early 2025, the price had moderately pulled back, settling around $115, reflecting overall substantial growth over the two-year period despite the mid-period dip.
