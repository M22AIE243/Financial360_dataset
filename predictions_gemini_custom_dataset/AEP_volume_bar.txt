The AEP Quarterly Volume chart illustrates trading activity from Q1 2023 through Q4 2024.

Volume generally increased throughout 2023, peaking significantly in Q1 2024 at approximately 250 million units. Following this peak, volume experienced a notable decline and then stabilized at a lower level for the remaining quarters of 2024, hovering around 180 million units. The lowest volume recorded was in Q2 2023, at roughly 175 million units.
