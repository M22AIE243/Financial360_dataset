The GL Quarterly Volume remained relatively stable throughout 2023, averaging around $30-40 million per quarter. A significant surge was observed in Q2 2024, with volume skyrocketing to over $200 million, marking the highest point in the period. Post-peak, volumes receded but remained elevated compared to 2023, settling around $60 million in Q3 2024 and approximately $45 million in Q4 2024.
