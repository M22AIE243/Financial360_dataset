The CMA Quarterly Volume experienced significant fluctuations, peaking dramatically in Q2 2023 at approximately 255 million units after strong growth in Q1 2023. This peak was followed by a sharp decline in Q3 2023, settling around 140 million units.

For the entirety of 2024, volumes have largely stabilized, consistently ranging between 130 and 145 million units per quarter. While stable, these recent quarterly volumes remain notably below the exceptional levels observed in the first half of 2023.
