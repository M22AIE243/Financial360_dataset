The BBWI stock price has exhibited significant volatility over the past two years, from early 2023 to early 2025.

After an initial decline through most of 2023, hitting lows near $28, the stock staged a robust rally in early 2024, surging past $50. However, this ascent was sharply reversed by mid-2024, bringing prices back to the $28-$30 range by late 2024.
