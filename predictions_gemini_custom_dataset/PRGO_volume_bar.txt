PRGO's quarterly volume experienced strong growth throughout 2023, rising from approximately 67 million units in Q1 2023 to over 110 million by Q4 2023. The volume peaked in Q1 2024 at over 120 million units before declining for the subsequent two quarters. While volumes dropped to around 87 million by Q3 2024, a modest recovery brought the Q4 2024 volume to roughly 95 million, remaining significantly higher than early 2023 levels.
