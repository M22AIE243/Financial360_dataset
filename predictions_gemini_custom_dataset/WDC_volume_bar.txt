WDC's quarterly volume demonstrated significant volatility from Q1 2023 to Q4 2024. Volume initially declined through 2023, hitting a low in Q3 2023, before experiencing a sharp rebound into year-end.

The peak volume of approximately 550 million units was recorded in Q1 2024, marking the highest point in the observed period. However, following this peak, a consistent downward trend was observed throughout 2024.

By Q4 2024, volume had decreased significantly from its Q1 peak, concluding the year at levels below both Q4 2023 and the 2024 high.
