This chart depicts WEC's quarterly volume from Q1 2023 through Q4 2024. Volume remained relatively stable around 84 million units during the first three quarters of 2023. A significant surge then occurred, peaking at approximately 140 million units in Q1 2024, representing a substantial increase. Subsequently, volumes showed some volatility, declining to about 119 million units by Q4 2024, though still markedly higher than early 2023 levels.
