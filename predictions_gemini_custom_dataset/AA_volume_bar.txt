This chart illustrates AA's quarterly volume from Q1 2023 through Q4 2024, with volumes generally ranging from approximately 280 million to nearly 400 million units.

After an initial dip in Q2 2023, volume trended higher through 2023 and most of 2024, peaking at nearly 400 million in Q3 2024. The lowest volume was recorded in Q2 2023 at around 280 million. However, the final quarter of 2024 saw a significant decline, bringing volume back to levels similar to mid-2023.
