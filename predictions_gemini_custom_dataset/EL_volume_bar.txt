The EL Quarterly Volume shows a significant upward trend over the observed period from Q1 2023 to Q4 2024. Starting at approximately 90 million units in Q1 2023, volume experienced consistent growth through 2023, reaching about 180 million by Q4 2023.

After a brief stabilization in Q1-Q2 2024, the latter half of 2024 saw accelerated growth, culminating in a peak volume of nearly 295 million units in Q4 2024. This represents a substantial increase in quarterly volume over the two-year span.
