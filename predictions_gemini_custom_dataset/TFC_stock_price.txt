The TFC stock price experienced a sharp decline in Q1 2023, falling from approximately $44 to the mid-$20s.
Following this drop, the stock traded within a volatile range between $25 and $32 through much of 2023.
A strong uptrend commenced in late 2023, leading to a significant recovery and new highs around $47 by late 2024, before a slight pull back into early 2025.
