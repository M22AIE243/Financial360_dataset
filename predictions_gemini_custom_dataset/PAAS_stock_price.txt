The PAAS stock price exhibited distinct phases over the period from early 2023 to early 2025. Initially, it experienced a downward trend, declining from approximately $16-$18 in early 2023 to a low around $12 by March 2024.

Following this, the stock embarked on a strong upward rally, rapidly increasing to above $20 and peaking near $26 in late 2024. As of early 2025, the price has seen a moderate pullback but remains robustly above its previous lows, settling around the $20 mark.
