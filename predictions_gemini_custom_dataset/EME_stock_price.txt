The EME stock price exhibited a strong upward trajectory from early 2023 to early 2025.
After trading relatively flat, ranging between approximately $140 and $220 for most of 2023, the stock initiated a sharp rally in late 2023.
This surge continued throughout 2024, pushing the price to a peak above $500 by late 2024, before experiencing a slight pullback.
