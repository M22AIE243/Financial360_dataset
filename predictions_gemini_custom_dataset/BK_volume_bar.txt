BK's quarterly volume demonstrated a significant initial peak around 370 million units in Q1 2023, followed by a general declining trend through mid-2024. The volume reached its lowest point of approximately 215 million units in Q2 2024.

However, Q3 2024 experienced a notable rebound, with volume increasing to about 260 million units. This recovery saw a slight dip in Q4 2024, settling at approximately 245 million units. Overall, while volume has not returned to early 2023 levels, the latter half of 2024 indicates a stabilization and recovery from the mid-year low.
