The FHN stock price began 2023 stable at around $22, but experienced a sharp decline in March-April, plummeting to lows near $9-$10.

Following this significant drop, the stock entered a gradual recovery phase through late 2023 and most of 2024, showing consistent upward momentum.

By late 2024, FHN's recovery accelerated, with the price surging past $20 and approaching its initial 2023 levels by early 2025.
