The RGLD stock price exhibited considerable volatility from early 2023 to early 2025. After an initial surge and subsequent decline in 2023, reaching a low around $100, the stock experienced a robust rally through most of 2024. This upward trend led to a peak above $150 by late 2024. However, the price has since undergone a significant correction, falling back to approximately $130-$132 by early 2025.
