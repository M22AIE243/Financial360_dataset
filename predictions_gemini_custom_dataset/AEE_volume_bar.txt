The AEE Quarterly Volume chart illustrates notable fluctuations in trading activity between Q1 2023 and Q4 2024.

Volume remained relatively stable around 80-90 million in early 2023 before experiencing a substantial surge, peaking at over 120 million in Q4 2023. Post-peak, volume gradually declined throughout 2024, with Q3 and Q4 2024 levels returning to figures similar to those observed at the beginning of 2023, around 90 million.
