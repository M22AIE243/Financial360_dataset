PNR's quarterly volume shows notable fluctuations across the eight quarters from Q1 2023 to Q4 2024. Volume peaked at approximately 105 million in Q1 2023 before a decline through Q3 2023, reaching a low of about 72 million.

A strong recovery was observed towards the end of 2023 and into Q1 2024, where volume nearly matched the initial peak at around 102 million. The remainder of 2024 saw moderate volatility, with volumes generally remaining above 85 million but not surpassing the Q1 2024 high, ending the period around 85 million in Q4 2024.
