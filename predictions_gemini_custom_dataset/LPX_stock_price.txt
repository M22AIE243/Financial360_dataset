LPX stock exhibited a strong overall upward trend from early 2023 to early 2025. After starting around $58-$60, it experienced volatility in 2023, dipping to approximately $50 by the end of the year.

However, the stock surged significantly throughout 2024, more than doubling from its 2023 low to reach a peak near $120 in late 2024. By early 2025, it had pulled back slightly to trade around $103-$104, still representing a substantial gain over the two-year period.
