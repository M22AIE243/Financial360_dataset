AG's quarterly volume showed a strong overall upward trend from Q1 2023 to Q4 2024.
After an initial volume of approximately 460 million in Q1 2023, there was a noticeable decline, reaching a low of roughly 290 million in Q3 2023.
However, volume rapidly recovered and showed sustained quarter-over-quarter growth through 2024, with only a minor dip in Q3 2024.
The period concluded with a record high volume exceeding 620 million units in Q4 2024, more than doubling the Q3 2023 low.
