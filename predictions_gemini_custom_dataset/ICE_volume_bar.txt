This chart depicts ICE Quarterly Volume, showing total trading volume fluctuations from Q1 2023 to Q4 2024. Volume initially dipped in Q2 2023 and Q2 2024, before recovering in subsequent quarters each year. The latter half of 2024 exhibited particularly strong growth, culminating in the period's highest volume in Q4 2024, reaching approximately 180 million units. This indicates an overall upward trend in trading activity, despite intermittent quarterly slowdowns.
