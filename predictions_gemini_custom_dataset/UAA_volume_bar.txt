UAA's quarterly volume experienced a notable upward trajectory from Q1 2023 to Q4 2024, despite initial declines. Volume generally trended downwards through 2023, reaching a low of approximately 400 million units in Q3 2023.

A significant recovery and growth phase began in 2024, with volume escalating sharply to a peak of around 880 million units in Q3 2024. While the latest quarter, Q4 2024, saw a moderation to about 670 million units, it remains substantially higher than any quarter in 2023, indicating robust overall growth.
