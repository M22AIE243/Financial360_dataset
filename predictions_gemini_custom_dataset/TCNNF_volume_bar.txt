TCNNF's quarterly volume displayed considerable fluctuation from Q1 2023 through Q4 2024. Volume in 2023 generally remained below 27 million, with a low point in Q2.

However, 2024 saw a strong upward trend, initially peaking at approximately 36 million in Q1.
