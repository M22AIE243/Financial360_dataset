GOOS stock has experienced a substantial downward trend over the past two years, beginning in early 2023. After peaking at approximately $24.50 in Q1 2023, the price consistently declined throughout 2023, reaching lows around $10 by year-end.

A moderate recovery in Q2 2024 saw the stock briefly return to the $14 range, but this was followed by another significant drop in the latter half of 2024. As of early 2025, the stock is trading near its two-year lows, stabilizing around the $10 mark.
