BLDR's Quarterly Volume exhibited significant fluctuations from Q1 2023 to Q4 2024, peaking at approximately 158 million in Q2 2023 and reaching its lowest point at 77 million in Q1 2024. Year-over-year, volumes in Q1, Q2, and Q4 2024 were notably lower than their respective 2023 quarters, although Q3 2024 saw a slight increase. The final quarter of 2024 concluded on a weak note, with volume declining significantly to about 82 million, representing a substantial drop from Q4 2023 and the lowest quarterly volume recorded in the latter half of 2024.
