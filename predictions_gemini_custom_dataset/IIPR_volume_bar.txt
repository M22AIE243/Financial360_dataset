IIPR's quarterly trading volume experienced a general decline from Q1 2023 through Q3 2024.
Starting from a high of over 22 million in Q1 2023, volume steadily decreased, reaching its lowest point around 11 million in Q3 2024.
However, the final quarter of 2024 witnessed a significant rebound, with volume surging back to nearly 19 million.
This marked increase in Q4 2024 represents the second-highest quarterly volume observed within the charted period.
