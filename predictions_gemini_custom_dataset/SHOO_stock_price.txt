The SHOO stock price exhibited significant growth from January 2023 to January 2025, rising from approximately $29 to around $42. After fluctuating within the low $30s for much of 2023, the stock initiated a strong rally in late 2023 and continued an upward trend through most of 2024. It reached a peak near $49 in October 2024. Subsequently, the stock experienced a noticeable correction, pulling back to trade around $42 as of early 2025.
