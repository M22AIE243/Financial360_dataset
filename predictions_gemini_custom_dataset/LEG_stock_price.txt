The LEG stock price experienced a significant and sustained downtrend from early 2023 to early 2025. Starting around $29-$30 in January 2023, and briefly peaking near $34, the price steadily decreased throughout 2023. A sharp drop in early 2024 saw the stock fall from approximately $26 to the $10-$12 range, where it largely consolidated for the remainder of the year. By early 2025, LEG shares closed near their lowest point observed in the period, around $9-$10.
