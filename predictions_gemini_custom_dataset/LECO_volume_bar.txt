LECO's quarterly volume demonstrated significant volatility between Q1 2023 and Q4 2024. Volume initially declined from a peak of approximately 25 million in Q1 2023, reaching a low of around 18 million by Q1 2024.

A robust rebound in Q2 2024 saw volume recover to nearly 24.5 million, but this recovery was not sustained. The latter half of 2024 indicated a renewed downward trend, with Q4 2024 volume concluding near the lower levels observed at the start of the year.
