The DY Quarterly Volume started strong in Q1 2023 at approximately 36 million units, then experienced a significant decline through the rest of 2023 and into Q1/Q2 2024, reaching its lowest point around 16.5 million. A clear recovery trend began in Q3 2024, culminating in a strong rebound to approximately 28 million units by Q4 2024. While this represents substantial growth from the lows, the volume in Q4 2024 still remains below the peak observed in Q1 2023.
