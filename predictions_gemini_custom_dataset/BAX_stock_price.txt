The BAX stock price displayed significant volatility from early 2023 to early 2025, starting near its peak around $49.

After an initial decline and recovery, the stock fluctuated broadly between $35 and $47 for much of 2023, hitting a low near $30 before recovering to the low $40s by mid-2024.

A pronounced downtrend began in late 2024, continuing into early 2025, bringing the price to its lowest point in the period, currently below $30.
