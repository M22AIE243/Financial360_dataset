The ES Quarterly Volume chart reveals notable fluctuations from Q1 2023 through Q4 2024. Volume experienced a consistent upward trend throughout 2023, culminating in a peak of nearly 200 million in Q1 2024.

Following this peak, Q2 2024 saw a sharp decline in activity. Volume subsequently stabilized in the latter half of 2024, remaining significantly below the Q1 2024 high but generally above the early 2023 levels.
