**INTC Stock Price Analysis**

The INTC stock price chart exhibits a notable trend over the observed period from January 2023 to January 2025.

*   **Peak and Decline (2024):** Following the peak, the stock price experienced a sharp decline to around $20 by mid-2024.
*   **Stabilization (2024-2025):** After the decline, the stock price stabilized and fluctuated between $20-$25, ending at around $20 in January 2025.

Overall, the stock price demonstrated a significant increase followed by a sharp decline and subsequent stabilization.
