The TJX Quarterly Volume chart illustrates trading activity from Q1 2023 through Q4 2024.
Volume remained relatively stable throughout 2023, generally hovering between 300 and 330 million shares per quarter.
A significant spike occurred in Q2 2024, pushing volume to its peak at approximately 380 million shares.
Following this peak, volume retreated, normalizing to levels consistent with the prior year's activity by Q3 and Q4 2024.
