The PPL stock price experienced notable volatility and a significant recovery over the past two years. After starting 2023 at approximately $27, it saw a decline, hitting a low of around $22 by October 2023. Subsequently, the stock entered a strong uptrend, rallying consistently throughout late 2023 and 2024 to reach a peak of over $34. As of early 2025, the price has undergone a modest correction, trading around $32.
