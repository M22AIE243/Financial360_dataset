The PNW Quarterly Volume displayed notable fluctuations from Q1 2023 to Q4 2024. Volume in 2023 dipped to its lowest point around 45 million in Q2 before recovering to approximately 60 million by year-end.

Q1 2024 experienced a significant surge, reaching the period's peak at nearly 88 million, followed by a decline in the subsequent two quarters. However, the volume concluded strongly in Q4 2024 at around 80 million, indicating robust year-over-year growth compared to Q4 2023.
