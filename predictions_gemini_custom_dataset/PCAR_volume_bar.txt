PCAR's quarterly volume experienced a significant decline throughout 2023, dropping from over 200 million units in Q1 to a low of approximately 142 million by Q4. The volume began to recover in 2024, showing steady growth from Q1 to a peak of about 182 million units in Q3. However, the final quarter of 2024 saw a decrease, closing at approximately 152 million units. Overall, while 2024 demonstrated a rebound from 2023's lows, volumes remained below the peak levels observed in early 2023.
