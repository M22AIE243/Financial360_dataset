VFC's quarterly volume displayed significant volatility from Q1 2023 through Q4 2024. After a dip to approximately 400 million in Q3 2023, volume recovered strongly, reaching a peak of nearly 590 million in Q2 2024. However, the latter half of 2024 experienced a declining trend, with Q4 2024 volume settling around 420 million. This represents a notable decrease from both the Q2 2024 peak and the corresponding Q4 2023 volume.
