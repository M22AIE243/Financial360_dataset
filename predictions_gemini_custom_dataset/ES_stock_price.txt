The ES Stock Price demonstrated significant volatility between early 2023 and early 2025. It began around $77 in January 2023, then experienced a sharp decline through the year, reaching a low near $49 by late October 2023.

A recovery followed, pushing the price to approximately $66 by September 2024, but this gain was partially reversed by a recent downturn. As of early 2025, the stock is trading around $56-$57, representing a notable overall decline from its initial peak.
