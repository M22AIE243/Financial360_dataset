The BBY Quarterly Volume demonstrated an overall increasing trend from Q1 2023 to Q4 2024. After an initial decline through the first three quarters of 2023, volume began a strong rebound in Q4 2023. It subsequently peaked in Q2 2024 at approximately 203 million units. While there was a slight dip in Q3 2024, volumes recovered slightly by Q4 2024, maintaining significantly higher levels than those observed in early 2023.
