The WDC stock price experienced significant growth from early 2023 to mid-2024, rising from approximately $24 to a peak near $60 by July 2024.

Following this robust rally, the stock underwent a sharp correction, dropping into the mid-$40s. It then traded largely sideways, fluctuating between $45 and $55.

By early 2025, the stock price settled around $45-$46, having lost a considerable portion of its gains from the peak.
