The KGC Quarterly Volume chart illustrates trading activity from Q1 2023 to Q4 2024. In 2023, volume initially declined before recovering towards the end of the year, though overall lower than the start. 2024, however, saw a significant surge, with peak volumes exceeding 1 billion in Q2 and Q3, marking the highest levels observed. While Q4 2024 showed a slight moderation, overall trading volume remained robust compared to 2023.
