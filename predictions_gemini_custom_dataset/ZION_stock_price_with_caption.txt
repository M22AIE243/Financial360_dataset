ZION stock experienced significant volatility from early 2023, initially falling sharply from around $45-$50 to a low of $18.31 by mid-2023.

Following this severe downturn, the stock demonstrated a strong and sustained recovery, steadily climbing throughout the latter half of 2023. This positive momentum continued robustly into 2024, with the price surpassing its pre-decline levels and reaching a peak of $61.25 by late 2024. The stock currently trades significantly higher than its early 2023 starting point, reflecting a powerful rebound.
