The RH stock price has displayed significant volatility and a notable recovery over the past two years. After rallying strongly to nearly $400 in mid-2023, it experienced a sharp correction, trading in a lower range through late 2023 and much of 2024. However, the stock initiated a robust upward trend in late 2024, continuing into early 2025. It has recently surged, reaching new highs near $450, indicating strong positive momentum.
