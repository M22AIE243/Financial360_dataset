The AES Quarterly Volume displayed a clear upward trend from Q1 2023 through Q4 2024. After an initial increase and a slight dip in Q3 2023, volume saw a significant surge in Q4 2023, stabilizing around 500 million units for the first three quarters of 2024. The highest quarterly volume was recorded in Q4 2024, exceeding 800 million units. This indicates robust growth, culminating in a strong year-end performance for AES.
