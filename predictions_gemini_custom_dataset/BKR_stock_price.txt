The BKR stock price has demonstrated a strong overall upward trend from early 2023 to early 2025, climbing from approximately $26 to over $40.

After an initial rally and a dip in early 2023, the stock saw significant gains reaching the mid-$30s by October 2023, followed by a notable decline into early 2024. The year 2024 marked a robust recovery and an accelerated rally, pushing the price to an approximate peak of $44.50. The stock has since experienced a slight pullback, currently trading around $40-$41 as of early 2025.
