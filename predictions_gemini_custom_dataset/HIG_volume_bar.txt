The HIG Quarterly Volume experienced notable fluctuations from Q1 2023 to Q4 2024. Volume peaked at approximately 140 million in Q1 2023, subsequently declining to a low of about 80 million by Q3 2023 before rebounding year-end.

Throughout 2024, volumes generally stabilized within a range of 90 million to 115 million per quarter. This indicates a moderation in activity compared to the exceptionally high start of 2023, with no clear sustained upward or downward trend across the 2-year period.
