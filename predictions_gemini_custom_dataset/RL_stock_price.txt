The RL stock price has demonstrated a strong bullish trend from January 2023 to January 2025. After consolidating largely within the $105-$125 range through much of 2023, the stock initiated a significant rally in late 2023. It then experienced a sharp surge in early 2024, peaking near $190, followed by a period of consolidation. The stock resumed its strong ascent in late 2024, currently trading above $220, effectively more than doubling its value over the two-year period.
