The ADP stock price demonstrated a strong bullish trend from January 2023 to January 2025, climbing from approximately $220 to over $290. After an initial dip in early 2023 and a more significant correction in late 2023 (around November), the stock recovered robustly. The most substantial gains occurred in the latter half of 2024, propelling the price above $300 to an observed peak before a slight consolidation.
