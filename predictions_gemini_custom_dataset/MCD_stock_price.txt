The MCD stock price displayed significant volatility over the past two years, moving from approximately $250 in early 2023 to around $290 by early 2025.

The stock experienced distinct rallies and corrections, notably a strong recovery from a low of around $240 in October 2023, leading to new highs near $290 by early 2024. Following a sharp decline to revisit the $240 support level by mid-2024, MCD staged another impressive rally.

This most recent surge propelled the stock to an observed peak above $310 in late 2024. However, it has since pulled back, currently consolidating around the $285-$290 range.
