IVZ quarterly trading volume exhibited notable volatility from Q1 2023 to Q4 2024. After an initial decline in early 2023, volume peaked in Q4 2023 at approximately 350 million units.

Subsequently, volume showed a consistent downward trend throughout 2024, reaching its lowest point of around 220 million units by Q4 2024. This indicates a significant reduction in trading activity by the end of the observed period compared to its peak and even its starting levels.
