AEO stock experienced significant volatility from early 2023 to early 2025. After an initial dip to just under $10 in mid-2023, the stock saw a robust rally, more than doubling to reach a peak of nearly $25 by April 2024. Subsequently, AEO's price has been on a downtrend, steadily declining through late 2024 and into early 2025, settling around the $16-$17 range.
