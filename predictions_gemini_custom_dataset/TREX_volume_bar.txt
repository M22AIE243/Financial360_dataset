This chart illustrates TREX's quarterly trading volume from Q1 2023 through Q4 2024. Volume experienced a gradual decline throughout 2023, reaching its lowest point in Q1 and Q2 2024 at around 5.1 million units.

However, the latter half of 2024 shows a significant and sharp rebound in activity. Volume surged to approximately 7.6 million in Q3 2024, culminating in the highest recorded volume of about 8.6 million units in Q4 2024, indicating a strong positive shift in market interest.
