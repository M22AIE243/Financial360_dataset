SCHW stock experienced a dramatic decline in early 2023, plummeting from over $80 to a low around $46 by Q2 2023. Following this sharp drop, the stock began a volatile but sustained recovery throughout late 2023 and 2024, gradually climbing back towards its previous highs. By late 2024, SCHW had largely recouped its losses, peaking again around $82, before a slight pullback to the mid-$70s by early 2025.
