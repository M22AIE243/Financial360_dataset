ZION's quarterly trading volume exhibited a clear peak in Q2 2023, reaching approximately 375 million units, following a strong start to the year. Volume then generally declined through Q4 2023, experiencing a minor rebound in Q1 2024. However, the latter half of 2024 showed a sustained and pronounced downtrend, with the lowest volume for the entire period recorded in Q4 2024 at roughly 85 million units.
