The WMT stock price chart from early 2023 to early 2025 demonstrates a strong overall bullish trend. After consolidating largely between $45 and $55 through much of 2023, the stock initiated a significant upward surge starting in late 2023. This trajectory led to a nearly doubling of its value, reaching highs above $90 by early 2025, with a minor pullback observed at the very end of the period.
