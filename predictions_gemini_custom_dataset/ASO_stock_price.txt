The ASO stock price exhibited significant volatility from January 2023 to January 2025. After an initial rally and subsequent dip to the low $40s by October 2023, the stock surged dramatically to a peak near $75 in early 2024.

Following this peak, the price underwent a sharp correction, falling back to the high $40s by late 2024. However, it has since shown a strong rebound, ending the period on a clear upward trajectory, currently trading around $60.
