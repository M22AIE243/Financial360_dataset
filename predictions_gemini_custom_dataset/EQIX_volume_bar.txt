The EQIX quarterly volume exhibited fluctuation throughout 2023, generally remaining between approximately 22.5 million and 28.5 million units. Volume then saw a significant increase into 2024, peaking at about 40 million units in Q2 2024, marking the highest point in the observed period.

Following this peak, volume experienced a notable decline in Q3 2024, recovering slightly to around 30.5 million units by Q4 2024. Despite the late-year drop, the overall volume trend in 2024, especially the first half, was considerably higher than that of 2023.
