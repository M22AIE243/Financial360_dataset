The COLM stock price experienced significant volatility from early 2023 to early 2025. After peaking near $94.50 in early 2023, the stock underwent a sharp decline, reaching a low of approximately $68.50 by October 2023.

A subsequent recovery through late 2023 established a largely range-bound trading pattern between $73 and $85 for most of 2024. The period concluded with a strong rally in late 2024 extending into early 2025, pushing prices back above $90 before a slight retracement.
