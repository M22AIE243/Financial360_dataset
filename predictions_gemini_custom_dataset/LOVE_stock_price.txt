The LOVE stock price exhibited significant volatility from January 2023 to January 2025. For much of 2023, the stock fluctuated between roughly $20 and $30, dipping to a low near $15 by late 2023 before recovering. In 2024, it largely traded between $20 and $30 again, experiencing multiple peaks and troughs.

A substantial rally commenced in late 2024, pushing the stock to its peak over $37, but this was quickly followed by a sharp correction back towards the mid-$20s by early 2025.
