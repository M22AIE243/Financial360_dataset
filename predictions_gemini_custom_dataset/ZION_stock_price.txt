ZION stock began 2023 around $45-$50, but experienced a severe downturn in March-April, plummeting below $20. Following this sharp decline, the stock entered a recovery phase throughout the latter half of 2023, gradually climbing back into the mid-$30s.

The positive momentum accelerated significantly through 2024, with the stock consistently gaining ground and reaching a peak above $60 by late 2024. While seeing a slight pullback from its peak into early 2025, ZION has largely recovered from its 2023 trough and shows strong appreciation over the two-year period.
