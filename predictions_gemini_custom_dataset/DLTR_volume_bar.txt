DLTR's quarterly volume remained relatively stable through 2023, consistently around 140 million units. A significant upward trend began in Q1 2024, with volume increasing to approximately 175 million. This growth accelerated sharply in the second half of 2024, culminating in record volumes exceeding 250 million units by Q4 2024. Overall, the company experienced substantial volume expansion in 2024, nearly doubling its early 2023 levels.
