The ZTS quarterly trading volume displayed varying trends from Q1 2023 to Q4 2024. Volumes in 2023 remained relatively stable, generally ranging between 110-135 million units. However, 2024 saw a significant surge, peaking at nearly 200 million in Q2, followed by a sharp decline in Q3 and a partial recovery in Q4 to approximately 150 million. Overall, 2024 experienced higher peak volumes but also greater volatility compared to 2023.
