The EIX Quarterly Volume chart displays significant fluctuations in trading activity from Q1 2023 to Q4 2024. Volumes saw a notable dip in mid-2023, with Q2 and Q3 volumes falling below 100 million units. The highest activity was recorded in Q4 2023 and Q1 2024, both exceeding 140 million units. After a slight drop in Q2 2024, the latter half of 2024 demonstrates a robust recovery and an upward trend in EIX volume.
