This chart illustrates VST's quarterly volume from Q1 2023 through Q4 2024. Volume in 2023 remained relatively stable, fluctuating between approximately 200 million and 300 million units per quarter.

A significant surge in activity began in 2024, with volume peaking dramatically above 500 million units in Q2 2024. Although volumes slightly moderated in Q3 and Q4 2024, they consistently remained well above the highest levels observed in 2023, indicating a strong overall growth trend.
