The LNC stock price experienced a sharp decline in Q1 2023, falling from initial levels above $30 to a low of approximately $17.50 by April 2023. Following this drop, the stock embarked on a robust recovery trend throughout the remainder of 2023 and into 2024. It achieved a peak above $35 in late 2024 before experiencing a slight pullback, settling around $30-$31 by early 2025.
