This bar chart illustrates CUBI's quarterly volume from Q1 2023 to Q4 2024.

Volume reached its peak in Q1 2023 at over 50 million units, followed by a sharp decline through the remainder of 2023, stabilizing around the 20-22 million mark by Q1 2024. A notable rebound occurred in Q3 2024, bringing volume to approximately 30 million. While Q4 2024 saw a slight dip to about 25 million, overall volumes remain significantly below the initial high of early 2023.
