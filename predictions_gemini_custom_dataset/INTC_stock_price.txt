INTC stock demonstrated a strong upward trend from early 2023, climbing from approximately $25-28 to peak near $50 by January 2024.
Following this peak, the stock experienced a significant and sharp decline through mid-2024, stabilizing briefly around $30-35 before another steep drop.

The price has since struggled to recover, oscillating in the low $20s and ending the observed period near its recent lows in early 2025.
