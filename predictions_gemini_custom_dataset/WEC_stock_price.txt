The WEC stock price experienced significant volatility from January 2023 to January 2025. After an initial decline, it reached a low of approximately $73 in late 2023 and consolidated in the $75-$85 range through mid-2024.

A strong rally began in the latter half of 2024, propelling the stock above $90 and peaking over $100 by December 2024. The price then saw a modest correction.

As of early 2025, WEC stock is trading around $94, reflecting a robust recovery and net appreciation over the two-year period.
