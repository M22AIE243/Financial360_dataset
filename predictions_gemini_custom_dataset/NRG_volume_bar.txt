The NRG Quarterly Volume experienced considerable fluctuations between Q1 2023 and Q4 2024. Volume peaked notably in Q2 2023 at approximately 315 million units before a sharp decline in Q3 2023.

Throughout 2024, the trend was generally downward, with each quarter recording lower volumes compared to its 2023 counterpart. This culminated in the lowest volume of approximately 168 million units recorded in Q4 2024, indicating a significant overall decrease from the 2023 peak.
