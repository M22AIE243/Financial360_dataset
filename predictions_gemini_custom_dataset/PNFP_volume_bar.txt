The PNFP quarterly volume shows a notable decline from Q1 2023 to Q2 2024, peaking at approximately 49 million in Q1 2023. This was followed by a consistent decrease, reaching its lowest point of about 25 million in Q2 2024.

Subsequently, the volume experienced a modest recovery in the latter half of 2024, projected to end the year around 32 million. While recovering from its trough, the projected Q4 2024 volume remains significantly lower than the levels observed in early 2023.
