The PEG stock price experienced a significant upward trend from early 2023 to early 2025.
During most of 2023, the stock traded largely sideways, fluctuating between approximately $55 and $62.
A strong bullish momentum began in late 2023, propelling the price to a peak above $90 by late 2024.
Although a slight pullback occurred in early 2025, the stock finished the period substantially higher, around $84.
