The STLD stock price displayed significant volatility from January 2023 to January 2025. After an initial surge and subsequent dip below $90 by mid-2023, the stock embarked on a strong upward trend, reaching a peak near $148 in April 2024.

Following a correction, it staged another powerful rally to hit a new all-time high above $150 in December 2024. However, the stock has since experienced a sharp decline, currently trading around the $113-$115 range as of early 2025.
