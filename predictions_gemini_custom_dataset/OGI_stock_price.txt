OGI stock experienced a significant decline through 2023, falling from nearly $4.00 in January to a low near $1.00 by year-end. Following this, the stock staged a robust recovery, peaking around $2.70 by Q1/Q2 2024. Subsequently, it has pulled back and appears to be consolidating, trading in the $1.50 to $1.60 range as of early 2025.
