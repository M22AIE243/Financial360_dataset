The CASH quarterly volume exhibited significant fluctuation from Q1 2023 through Q4 2024. Starting at a high of approximately 18 million in Q1 2023, volumes generally declined, reaching a low of about 10 million by Q2 2024.

Subsequently, there was a modest recovery, with volumes rising to nearly 13 million by the end of 2024. Despite this recent uptick, the closing volume for Q4 2024 remains substantially below the peak observed at the start of the period.
