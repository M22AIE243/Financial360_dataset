This chart displays MS's quarterly trading volume from Q1 2023 through Q4 2024, with volumes scaled in hundreds of millions.

Volume reached its highest points in Q4 2023 and Q1 2024, both exceeding 550 million. Following these peaks, there was a notable decline in mid-2024, with Q3 2024 recording the lowest volume around 390 million. Although Q4 2024 saw a slight rebound, it remained significantly lower than the preceding year's peak performance.
