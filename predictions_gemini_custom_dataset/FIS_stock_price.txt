FIS stock experienced significant volatility in early 2023, sharply declining from over $70 to approximately $47 by April. Following this steep drop, the stock embarked on a strong and sustained recovery, trending upwards from late 2023 through most of 2024. It peaked near $90 in late 2024, demonstrating substantial growth from its lows. As of early 2025, the price has seen a modest pullback, currently trading around $80-$82.
