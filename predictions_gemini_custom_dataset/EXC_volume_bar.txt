EXC's quarterly volume exhibited notable fluctuation from Q1 2023 through Q4 2024. Volume initially dipped from Q1 to Q3 2023, then surged to a peak of nearly 500 million units in Q4 2023. Subsequently, 2024 shows a period of relatively stable, high volume. Although starting strong, volumes experienced a slight gradual decline from Q1 through Q4 2024, settling around 400 million units by year-end.
