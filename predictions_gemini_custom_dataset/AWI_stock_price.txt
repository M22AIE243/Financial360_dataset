The AWI stock price has exhibited a strong bullish trend from January 2023 to early 2025. After an initial period of fluctuation in 2023, the stock saw a significant rally from approximately $70 in late 2023 to over $120 by early 2024.

Following a period of consolidation, the stock experienced another substantial surge in the latter half of 2024, peaking above $160. Currently, in early 2025, the price has pulled back, settling in the $140-$145 range, but still significantly higher than its starting point.
