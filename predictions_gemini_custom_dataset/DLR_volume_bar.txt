The DLR Quarterly Volume chart illustrates fluctuating trading activity from Q1 2023 to Q4 2024. Total volume peaked significantly in Q2 2023, reaching nearly 190 million, which was the highest across the period.

Following this peak, volumes generally declined through late 2023 and much of 2024, hitting a low of approximately 105 million in Q3 2024. The latest quarter, Q4 2024, shows a modest recovery in volume, but it remains below the levels observed in early 2023.
