CGC stock price demonstrated a clear downward trend over the two-year period from early 2023 to early 2025. After peaking above $30 in Q1 2023, the stock experienced a steep and sustained decline, falling below $5 by mid-2023.

Despite brief, sharp rallies in October 2023 and May 2024, which saw prices rebound to approximately $17.50 and $15 respectively, these gains were not sustained. The stock continued its overall descent, ending near its lowest point around $2.50-$3 by January 2025.
