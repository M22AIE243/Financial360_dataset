OGI's quarterly volume demonstrated significant volatility from Q1 2023 to Q4 2024. Volume initially grew strongly from approximately 15 million in Q1 2023, peaking at an impressive 83 million in Q2 2024. Subsequently, the latter half of 2024 experienced a sharp decline, with volume falling to around 41 million by the year's end. This pattern indicates a period of robust engagement followed by a substantial pullback in activity.
